# Healthcare Recipe Management Platform – Business Analysis Case Study

## Overview

This repository showcases an end-to-end Business Analysis case study for a Healthcare Recipe Management Platform designed for an Inflammatory Bowel Disease (IBD) non-profit organisation.

The project focuses on improving patient engagement by providing a centralised platform where patients, caregivers, healthcare professionals, and contributors can discover, submit, review, and share diet-specific recipes tailored to IBD-related needs.

The case study demonstrates the application of Business Analysis techniques throughout the project lifecycle, from problem identification and stakeholder analysis to requirements gathering, backlog creation, user story development, process modelling, prioritisation, and solution design.

## Business Problem

Patients were being redirected to external websites and resources for recipe recommendations, resulting in:

* Fragmented user experience
* Limited engagement with the organisation's platform
* Lack of trusted dietary-specific content
* No standard process for recipe contribution and approval

## Project Objectives

* Increase user engagement through a dedicated recipe platform
* Improve access to trusted dietary-specific recipes
* Enable community-generated content through recipe submissions
* Establish a structured recipe review and approval process
* Support patients and caregivers with nutritional information and dietary filtering
